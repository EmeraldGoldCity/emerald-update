## 🎨 Logo Styling Enhancements

### What Changed:

**Before:**
- Logo with white background directly on green header
- Created visual disconnect
- Looked less polished

**After:**
- ✅ White rounded container with shadow
- ✅ Elegant padding around logo
- ✅ Seamless integration with green header
- ✅ Professional, modern appearance

### Technical Implementation:

```tsx
<div className="bg-white rounded-lg px-3 py-2 shadow-md">
  <img 
    src={logo} 
    alt="Emerald City Limos" 
    className="h-12 w-auto object-contain"
  />
</div>
```

**Styling Details:**
- **Container**: `bg-white` (white background)
- **Corners**: `rounded-lg` (smooth rounded corners)
- **Padding**: `px-3 py-2` (comfortable spacing)
- **Elevation**: `shadow-md` (subtle drop shadow)
- **Logo Height**: `h-12` (48px - slightly reduced for better proportion)
- **Aspect Ratio**: `w-auto object-contain` (maintains original proportions)

### Visual Benefits:

1. **🎯 Clear Brand Identity** - Logo stands out prominently
2. **✨ Modern Design** - Clean, professional appearance
3. **🌈 Better Contrast** - White container pops against emerald green
4. **📱 Responsive** - Scales beautifully on all devices
5. **💎 Premium Feel** - Matches luxury brand positioning
